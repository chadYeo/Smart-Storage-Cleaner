Privacy Policy
Smart Storage · Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Smart Storage app ("Smart Storage", "the app", "we", "us") does and does not collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. Smart Storage is a phone storage cleaner. Our whole product is built so that the analysis of your files happens on your device and your personal content stays with you.

Our privacy pledge. Smart Storage analyzes your photos, videos and files 100% on your device. We do not require an account, we do not request your location, and we never upload, sell, or share the contents of your files with anyone.
1. Information we do NOT collect
Your photos, videos, documents, and file contents. All scanning, duplicate detection (SHA-256 and perceptual hashing), similar-photo grouping, on-device text recognition, best-shot scoring, and storage analysis run locally on your device. These files and their contents are never transmitted to us or to any third party.
Location. The app does not request or access your location.
Accounts or identity. There is no sign-up or login. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, messages, or call logs.
2. Information that is collected, and why
Smart Storage uses a small number of Google services. Through these services, limited data is processed as described below.

Purpose	Data involved	Provider
Advertising (free version only; disabled for Pro users)	Advertising ID, IP address, device information, and ad-interaction data used to serve and measure ads.	Google AdMob
Purchases (Smart Storage Pro)	Google processes your purchase. The app stores only a local flag indicating Pro is active; we do not receive or store your payment details.	Google Play Billing
Stability & diagnostics	Crash reports, diagnostic logs, and anonymous app-interaction events (for example, that a scan completed or how much space was freed). No file contents and no personal identity are included.	Google Play services / Firebase (crash & analytics)
We use this data only to keep the app running, to show ads that fund the free version, and to fix bugs and improve features. We do not sell your personal information.

Advertising choices & consent
In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and where required by U.S. state privacy laws, the app shows a consent dialog (Google's User Messaging Platform) that lets you choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. You can reset or limit ad personalization at any time in your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads. Purchasing Smart Storage Pro removes ads entirely.

Google's handling of advertising and analytics data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3. Permissions and how they are used
Smart Storage requests only the permissions needed to find and clean what is wasting your space. Each is used solely for the feature described.

Permission	Why it is used
Photos & videos / media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE on older Android)	To scan and show your photos and videos so you can review duplicates, similar shots, screenshots, and large media on your device.
All files access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)	To find junk files, leftover folders from uninstalled apps, downloads and installers, to analyze full storage, and to move deleted items into Trash. If you decline it, the app still works in a reduced, media-only mode. Access is used only for cleaning features.
Usage access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) — optional, granted by you in system settings	To show when apps were last used in the App manager so you can find ones you no longer open.
App list (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES) and uninstall (REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES)	To list installed apps and their sizes, detect leftover data from removed apps, and launch the system uninstall dialog when you choose to remove an app (you confirm every uninstall).
Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) and foreground service	To show progress while compressing a video.
Internet / network state (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)	To load ads and complete in-app purchases. No files or file contents are uploaded.
4. Data sharing
We do not sell your personal information and we do not share the contents of your files with anyone. The limited data in Section 2 is processed by Google in its role as our advertising, billing, and diagnostics provider. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Data retention and deletion
Items you delete in the app move to Smart Storage Trash (or your device's system trash) and are recoverable for 30 days, after which they are permanently removed. You can restore or permanently delete them at any time from the Trash screen.
On-device data (such as the local index of file hashes used to speed up rescans, and your Pro flag) stays on your device and is removed when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.
Diagnostics and advertising data are retained by Google according to their policies. To request deletion of data associated with the app, contact us at the email below and we will act on your request.
6. Children's privacy
Smart Storage is a general-utility app and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it.

7. Your rights
Depending on where you live (for example under the GDPR or the CCPA/CPRA and similar U.S. state laws), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data, and to opt out of personalized advertising or "sale/sharing" of personal information. Because the app has no account, most of your data never leaves your device and is controlled by you directly. For requests involving advertising or diagnostics data, contact us using the details below or use the ad-personalization controls in your device settings.

8. Security
Because your files are processed on your device and are not uploaded, they are protected by your device's own security. Network connections used for ads and purchases are encrypted in transit.

9. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
